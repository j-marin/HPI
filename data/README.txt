In this folder the folowing data are available: 
- Phylogenetic tree (FastTree) of E. coli: core_ecoli_rooted.nwk
- Dated tree (TDR method) of E. coli: TDR_tree.nwk 
- Dated tree (TDR method) of E. coli using CI for rate correction: TDR_tree_lowerCI.nwk and TDR_tree_upperCI.nwk and 
- Dated tree (LSD method) of E. coli: lsd2_dating_core.date.nexus
- Dataset metadata : typing.txt and recapHPI.csv
